## Log In to S.EE

1. Go to the [Login page](https://s.ee/user/login/)
2. Enter your email (or username) and password
3. Click **Log In**

## Third-Party Login

If you registered using Google or GitHub, click the corresponding button on the [login page](https://s.ee/user/login/) to sign in with that service.
