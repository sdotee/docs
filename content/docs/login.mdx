## Log In

1. Go to the [Login page](https://s.ee/user/login/)
2. Enter your email (or username) and password
3. Click **Log In**

---

## Third-Party Login

You can also sign in with Google or GitHub. Click the corresponding button on the [login page](https://s.ee/user/login/).

---

## Forgot Password?

1. Go to the [Password Reset page](https://s.ee/user/password/reset/)
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

---
