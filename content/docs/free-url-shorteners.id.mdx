S.EE menyediakan beberapa layanan pemendekan URL gratis untuk developer. Layanan ini tidak memerlukan registrasi. Semua tautan pendek yang dibuat bersifat permanen.

---

## Layanan yang Tersedia

| Service | URL | Supported Domains |
|---------|-----|-------------------|
| ba.sh | [https://app.ba.sh/](https://app.ba.sh/) | `github.com`, `gitlab.com`, `githubusercontent.com` |
| z.sh | [https://app.z.sh/](https://app.z.sh/) | `github.com`, `gitlab.com`, `githubusercontent.com` |
| kak.ao | [https://app.kak.ao/](https://app.kak.ao/) | `kakao.com`, `kakaotalk.com`, `kakaocorp.com` |

---

## Keterbatasan

| Feature | Free Services | S.EE Membership |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| Analytics | Not available | Full statistics |
| Custom slugs | Not available | Fully customizable |
| Supported domains | Limited | Any domain |
| Registration | Not required | Required |

<Callout type="info">
Jika Anda membutuhkan analitik, slug kustom, atau dukungan untuk domain apa pun, pertimbangkan untuk upgrade ke [keanggotaan S.EE](https://s.ee/user/pricing/).
</Callout>
