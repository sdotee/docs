## Create Your Account

1. Go to the [Sign Up page](https://s.ee/user/register/)
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Check your inbox for a verification email
4. Click the verification link to activate your account

After verifying your email, choose a plan that fits your needs. You can compare plans on the [pricing page](https://s.ee/user/pricing/).
